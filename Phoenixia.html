Phoenixia is a tabletop roleplaying setting for Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition that I have been making for the past three years. It is a mythic dark fantasy setting that takes place across multiple planets in the Caela Solar System. Each planet has their own species and culture, ranging from the aquatic wariors from Sulcor'al, to the raging wars within New Twilight, or even the near apocolyptic planet of the Foundry. There are many things that are going to be in the final product, such as 7 new subclasses, 11 new races, 2 custom lineages, and many magic items. I've been using AlchemyRPG as a home base for where all of the lore is contained, and is still heavily work in progress.
